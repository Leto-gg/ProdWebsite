About Us

Leto helps developers grow and protect their systems without collecting vast amounts of unnecessary user data.

Optimize Performance and Secure Your Applications

Our suite of tools offer real-time monitoring, performance tracking, and malware detection across various storage and retrieval systems. Enable robust defenses in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

IPFS and Filecoin enable verifiable, self soverign systems that provide resilient data access.

The analytics used in these systems must provide a similar model of privacy and resiliency.

The open source development of these tools will ensure Leto remains transparent and accountable to the open web.

Connecting your system with Leto

  • IPFS Gateway
    Serve content to end-users with the Leto IPFS Gateway and generate anonymous, open analytics in our Dashboard and Analytics API.
  • Open Analytics API
    Anyone can query our API to see how many times an IPFS object was requested through the network.
  • Malware Detection
    Use the automated Large Field Analyzer to quickly identify malware hosted by users on your platform or marketplace. Learn more in the docs.
  • Engage with Us
    Contribute to the future of analytics. Reach out on Email, or @Letodev on Twitter.
